What is
the Cautery Procedure?

The Cautery Procedure is a safe and minimally invasive treatment that uses heat to remove or modify skin tissues. It is commonly employed to address a range of conditions, including: Improving the appearance of scars by smoothing the tissue and reducing their Prominence and Eliminating the various of skin lesions, such as moles, warts, skin tags, and seborrheic keratosis.
