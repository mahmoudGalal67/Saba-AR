Fractional laser

What is
Fractional Laser Treatment?

Fractional Laser Treatment is a skin resurfacing technique that uses a laser to destroy damaged cells and promote the production of new ones. It helps restore the skin's youthfulness by promoting the synthesis of elastin and collagen.

What are the benefits of Fractional Laser Treatment at Sabaa Medical Center?

Fractional Laser treatment at “Sabaa Medical Center” offers you a wide range of benefits that make it your best choice. Here are the key advantages:
1- Fractional Laser targets deep skin layers, stimulating cellular renewal for improved texture, reduced wrinkles and scars, and even skin tone.
2- Fractional Laser treats acne, wound, and surgical scars by stimulating collagen and elastin production, fading scars and enhancing skin texture.
3- Fractional Laser effectively reduces dark spots, freckles, and age-related discoloration by breaking down excess pigment and renewing skin cells.
4- Fractional Laser treatment at Sabaa Medical Center requires minimal preparation and downtime, allowing for quick and efficient procedures without long recovery periods.
