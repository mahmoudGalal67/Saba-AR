Botox

What is
Botox ?

Botox, short for Botulinum toxin, is a popular cosmetic treatment used to temporarily reduce the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles on the face. It works by blocking nerve signals in the muscles where it is injected, causing those muscles to relax. This relaxation of the muscles smoothens out the overlying skin and reduces the visibility of wrinkles.

What is Botox used for?

Botox is commonly used for:
1. Frown Lines: Also known as "11 lines" or "glabellar lines," these are the vertical lines that appear between the eyebrows.
2. Crow's Feet: These are the lines that form at the outer corners of the eyes, often caused by smiling or squinting.
3. Forehead Lines: Horizontal lines that appear on the forehead due to various facial expressions.
4. Brow Lift: Botox can be strategically injected to give a slight lift to the eyebrows, creating a more open and youthful appearance.
5. Other Facial Wrinkles: Botox can be used to target wrinkles in various areas of the face, such as around the mouth and nose.
