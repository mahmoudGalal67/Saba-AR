Dermapen

What is
Dermapen ?

Dermapen is an advanced micro-needling treatment that uses fine needles to create controlled micro-injuries, stimulating collagen and elastin production for improved skin texture and reduced scars. Effective for concerns like wrinkles, acne scars, and uneven tone.

How Dermapen Works?

Dermapen employs the principles of traditional Chinese acupuncture to enhance skin appearance. Its mechanism involves 12 needles gently puncturing the skin's surface as the device glides over the treated area. The motor-driven movement of the needles stimulates the skin's natural healing process, triggering collagen and elastin production. These micro-injuries prompt the skin to rejuvenate, resulting in a more youthful look.

6 Benefits of Dermapen:

1. Dermapen stimulates collagen and elastin production, enhancing skin texture and reducing the appearance of scars and wrinkles.
2. It addresses diverse issues like fine lines, acne scars, and uneven tone, providing comprehensive skin improvement.
3. Dermapen offers results without the need for surgery, making it a gentle yet effective option for skin enhancement.
4. With limited recovery time, Dermapen allows you to resume regular activities sooner.
5. By promoting skin's natural healing, Dermapen leads to a tighter, more youthful appearance.
6. Customized Approach: Tailored treatments cater to individual skin needs, ensuring personalized results.
